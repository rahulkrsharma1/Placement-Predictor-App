About Campus Recruitment

Campus recruitment is a strategy for sourcing, engaging and hiring young talent for internship and entry-level positions. College recruiting is typically a tactic for medium- to large-sized companies with high-volume recruiting needs.

The dataset is collected from Kaggle website. Here is the link for the dataset. The goal of this project is to analyze the factors that can affect campus recruitment and create a model to predict placement chances.
