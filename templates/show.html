Tips to Crack Campus Placement Interview

You've done the hard work in your degree course. Now it's time to cross the last hurdle between you and your career â€“ campus placements interview.

  • Pay Attention to Pre-Placement Presentations

    Before the placement rounds start, each company's representative gives a presentation about their company. Pay attention to this presentation as you might be asked about it during the interview.

  • Do Mock Interviews

    Your college may prepare mock interviews, or you can take the initiative and conduct mock interviews with friends. Find mock interview questions online and take this exercise seriously.

  • Be on Time

    Arrive at least half an hour early to the venue of the interview. This ensures timeliness and gives you time for last-minute preparation.

  • Be Presentable

    A good outward appearance shows that you care about how you look. Make sure your hair and nails are cut, clothes are cleaned and ironed, and shoes are polished.

  • Be Confident But Not Overconfident

    Confidence is a quality all companies look for. Know the difference between confidence and overconfidence. Don't be brash.
